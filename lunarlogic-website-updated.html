One platform, complete AR automation

LunarLogic automates your entire accounts receivable workflow—from invoice creation through cash collection and financial reporting. Built for professional services firms that need enterprise capabilities without enterprise complexity.

Eliminate manual billing

Stop manually creating invoices, chasing payments, and processing sales orders. Automation handles routine AR tasks while your team focuses on client relationships and strategic work.

Accelerate cash collection

Automated reminder sequences and predictive analytics ensure invoices get paid faster. Average DSO reduction of 15-20 days translates to significant working capital improvement.

Gain real-time visibility

Live AR dashboard shows exactly where your receivables stand at any moment. Payment predictions and collection priorities tell you where to focus efforts for maximum impact.

Four integrated modules, one seamless platform

Deploy individual modules or the complete AR automation suite. Each module integrates with QuickBooks and works independently or as part of the full platform.
